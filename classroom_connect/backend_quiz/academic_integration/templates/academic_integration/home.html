About the Platform

Classroom Connect brings together quiz-taking capabilities with complete academic record management. Teachers can create interactive quizzes linked to tutorial assessments, while students can access all their academic information and attempt quizzes in one place.

For Students

  • Take course quizzes
  • View academic performance
  • Access tutorial, CA & assignment marks
  • Track progress across semesters

For Teachers

  • Create interactive quizzes
  • Manage course rosters
  • Track student performance
  • Auto-sync quiz scores to academic records
